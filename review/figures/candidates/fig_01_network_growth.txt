Figure 1 — Cumulative FLUXNET network growth by IGBP class (1990–2025). Stacked area shows cumulative number of sites by IGBP class based on first_year from the FLUXNET Shuttle snapshot (April 2026, 672 sites). Right Y axis shows percentage of functionally active sites — defined as sites with valid NEE data (≥3 months from MM data, or valid annual NEE from YY data) submitted within 4 years of the reference year. Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle snapshot v0.3.7, April 2026.
