Figure 02 — Deployment duration profile of the FLUXNET network
===============================================================

Layout: Three panels stacked vertically in a single column (vA layout).
        All panels share the same x-axis (record length in years) and y-axis
        (site count) limits, derived from the 2025 snapshot so scales are
        directly comparable across panels.

Panels (top to bottom):
  1. 2007 — La Thuile release snapshot
     Sites included in the FLUXNET La Thuile dataset. Histogram of record
     length (snapshot_year - first_year) at the time of the 2007 snapshot.

  2. 2015 — FLUXNET2015 release snapshot
     Sites included in the FLUXNET2015 dataset. Same record-length metric
     computed at 2015.

  3. 2025 — Shuttle/modern snapshot
     All sites available through the FLUXNET Shuttle as of 2025. The
     distribution extends furthest right, reflecting the growth in long-record
     sites over the intervening decade.

Fill colours:
  Blue  (#2196F3) — Functionally active: last_year >= snapshot_year - 4
  Grey  (#B0BEC5) — Inactive / high latency: last_year < snapshot_year - 4

Axis labels:
  X axis: "Record length (years)" — shown on the bottom panel only.
  Y axis: "Sites" — shared label outside the panel stack (left margin).

Annotations:
  Each panel carries a bold inset label (top-left) showing the snapshot year
  and site count, e.g. "2007 (n=279)".

Legend:
  Active/inactive legend positioned inside the top panel (top-left area).
  Hidden on panels 2 and 3 to avoid redundancy.

Source: FLUXNET Shuttle snapshot fluxnet_shuttle_snapshot_20260414T154430.csv
        Generated by fig_network_duration_profile() in R/figures/fig_network_growth.R
        Default years argument: c(2007, 2015, 2025)
        active_threshold = 4 years
