Figure 2 — FLUXNET Network Record Length: Historical Datasets vs Shuttle Snapshots

Three-panel figure comparing record length distributions between historical FLUXNET
datasets (outline, red) and Shuttle snapshots at the same time point (filled grey):
- Top panel: Marconi 2000 vs Shuttle snapshot 2000
- Middle panel: La Thuile 2007 vs Shuttle snapshot 2007
- Bottom panel: FLUXNET2015 vs Shuttle snapshot 2015

Record length = snapshot_year - first_year for each site. 1-year bins. X axis
clipped to 0-20 years to use full width. Y axis shared across all panels.

The grey filled bars show what the Shuttle contains at each historical time point.
The red outline bars show what was actually published in each historical release.
The gap between them reflects sites operating at the time that were not included
in the historical release due to data policy or processing constraints.

Data sources: Marconi (Falge et al. 2001, 35 sites), La Thuile 2007 (252 sites),
FLUXNET2015 (Pastorello et al. 2020, 212 sites), FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7 April 2026.
Function: fig_duration_overlay() in R/figures/fig_network_growth.R
Script: scripts/generate_duration_histograms.R
