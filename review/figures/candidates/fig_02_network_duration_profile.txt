Figure 2 — FLUXNET network record length distribution at four snapshot years (2000/2007/2015/2025), corresponding to the Marconi, La Thuile, FLUXNET2015, and Shuttle/modern datasets. Four panels stacked vertically, shared axes. Record length = snapshot_year - first_year for each site active by that year. Sites classified as Functionally active (blue) if they have valid NEE data within 4 years of the snapshot year, or Inactive/high latency (grey) otherwise. Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle snapshot v0.3.7, April 2026.
