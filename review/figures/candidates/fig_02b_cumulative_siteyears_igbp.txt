Figure 2b — Cumulative FLUXNET Site-Years by IGBP Class with Historical Dataset Overlays

Stacked area chart showing cumulative site-years accumulated by the FLUXNET Shuttle
network from 1991 to 2024, coloured by IGBP ecosystem class. Each year's contribution
is the count of Shuttle sites with valid data in that year (≥3 months valid NEE from
monthly data, or valid annual NEE) summed cumulatively.

Overlaid lines show cumulative site-years contributed by each historical dataset:
- Marconi (green): 35 sites, 97 site-years, active 1992-2000
- La Thuile (red): 252 sites, 965 site-years, active 1991-2007
- FLUXNET2015 (blue): 212 sites, 1532 site-years, active 1991-2014

Vertical dashed lines mark the publication year of each historical release (2000,
2007, 2015). The gap between the historical lines and the Shuttle background at
each release year shows the additional data the Shuttle captures retrospectively.

IGBP colours from plot_constants.R. Functionally active site-years from
data/snapshots/site_year_data_presence.csv.

Data sources: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7 April 2026; Marconi (Falge et al. 2001);
La Thuile 2007; FLUXNET2015 (Pastorello et al. 2020).
Function: fig_cumulative_siteyears_igbp() in R/figures/fig_network_growth.R
Script: scripts/generate_duration_histograms.R
