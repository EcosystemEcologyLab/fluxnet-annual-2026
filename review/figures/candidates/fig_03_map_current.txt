Figure 3 — FLUXNET Shuttle 2025: current network site locations

Global map showing all 759 sites in the current FLUXNET Shuttle snapshot
(June 2026) as individual point locations. Each point represents one flux
tower site. No spatial aggregation or regional binning is applied.

Points: filled circles (Okabe-Ito blue, #0072B2), black outline, uniform
size. White outline-only basemap with country borders from Natural Earth
(1:50m scale).

Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, snapshot 2026-06-01 (759 sites).
Function: fig_map_point_network() in R/figures/fig_maps.R
Script: scripts/generate_point_maps.R
