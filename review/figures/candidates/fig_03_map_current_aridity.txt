Figure 3 (aridity variant) — FLUXNET Shuttle 2025: current network sites
on CGIAR Global Aridity Index backdrop

Same site locations as the white-background variant. The backdrop shows the
CGIAR Global Aridity Index v3.1 (Zomer et al. 2022) binned into five UNEP
aridity classes:

  Hyper-Arid    (AI < 0.05)   #d73027 (red)
  Arid          (AI 0.05–0.20) #fc8d59 (orange)
  Semi-Arid     (AI 0.20–0.50) #ffff33 (yellow)
  Dry Sub-Humid (AI 0.50–0.65) #66bd63 (green)
  Humid         (AI ≥ 0.65)   white (no colour)

Raw AI values are stored as integers (AI × 10000); applied scaling factor
0.0001 per Zomer et al. (2022). Raster aggregated to ~0.2-degree resolution
for display. Country outlines (Natural Earth 1:50m) are rendered above the
aridity layer.

Data sources:
- Flux sites: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, snapshot 2026-06-01 (759 sites)
- Aridity: CGIAR Global Aridity Index v3.1 (Zomer, R.J.; Trabucco, A.; Bossio,
  D.A.; Verchot, L.V. 2022. Version 3.1. figshare. doi:10.6084/m9.figshare.7504448)
