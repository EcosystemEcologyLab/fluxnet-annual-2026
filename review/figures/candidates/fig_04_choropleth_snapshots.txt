Figure 4 — FLUXNET network site density by UN subregion at three time snapshots corresponding to major data releases: La Thuile (2007), FLUXNET2015 (2015), and the modern Shuttle dataset (2025). Fill colour shows number of sites established by each snapshot year (first_year <= snapshot_year). All three panels use identical colour scale breaks computed from the 2025 panel (maximum values) to allow direct visual comparison. Site locations shown as white-filled points with black outlines. UN subregion boundaries from Natural Earth via rnaturalearth R package. Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle snapshot v0.3.7, April 2026.
