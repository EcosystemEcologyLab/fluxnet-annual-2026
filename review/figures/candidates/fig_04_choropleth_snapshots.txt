Figure 4 — FLUXNET network site density by UN subregion for four historical datasets: Marconi 2000 (35 sites), La Thuile 2007 (252 sites), FLUXNET2015 (212 sites), and FLUXNET Shuttle 2025 (672 sites). Each panel uses the authoritative site list for that dataset. For sites present in the Shuttle, Shuttle coordinates and metadata are used. For sites not in the Shuttle, historical metadata is used as fallback. Shared colour scale across all panels. Data sources: Marconi (Falge et al. 2001), La Thuile dataset, FLUXNET2015 (Pastorello et al. 2020), FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7.
