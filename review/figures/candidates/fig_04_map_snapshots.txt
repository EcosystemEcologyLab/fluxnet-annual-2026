Figure 4 — FLUXNET network growth: four historical datasets as point maps

Four-panel global point map showing site locations for four successive FLUXNET
datasets. Each panel uses the authoritative site list for that dataset:

  Marconi 2000     — 35 sites   (Falge et al. 2001; comparison figure)
  La Thuile 2007   — 252 sites  (La Thuile dataset; comparison figure)
  FLUXNET2015      — 212 sites  (Pastorello et al. 2020; comparison figure)
  Shuttle 2025     — 759 sites  (FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, snapshot 2026-06-01)

Panels labelled "(comparison — non-Shuttle)" use historical site lists that
are not part of the primary Shuttle-based analysis (see CLAUDE.md §1).
No spatial aggregation or regional binning is applied to any panel.

Points: filled circles (Okabe-Ito blue, #0072B2), black outline, uniform size.
White outline-only basemap with country borders from Natural Earth (1:50m).

Data sources:
- Marconi: Falge, E. et al. (2001a,b). Agricultural and Forest Meteorology 107.
- La Thuile: La Thuile FLUXNET dataset (2007).
- FLUXNET2015: Pastorello, G. et al. (2020). Scientific Data 7: 225.
- Shuttle: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7.

Function: fig_map_point_snapshots() in R/figures/fig_maps.R
Script: scripts/generate_point_maps.R
