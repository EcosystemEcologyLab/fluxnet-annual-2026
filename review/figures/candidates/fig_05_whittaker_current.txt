Figure 5 — FLUXNET Network Coverage of Whittaker Biome Climate Space (Current Network)

This figure shows the distribution of all 530 FLUXNET sites with valid NEE data
in Whittaker biome climate space.

X axis: Mean Annual Temperature (°C) from WorldClim v2.1 bioclimatic variable
bio1 (1970-2000 baseline, 2.5 arc-minute resolution, Fick & Hijmans 2017).

Y axis: Mean Annual Precipitation (mm yr⁻¹) from WorldClim v2.1 bioclimatic
variable bio12 (same source).

Hexagonal bins show the median annual NEE_VUT_REF (g C m⁻² yr⁻¹) across all
site-years within each climate bin. Positive values indicate net carbon source,
negative values indicate net carbon sink. The diverging colour scale is centred
on zero and clipped symmetrically to the 5th-95th percentile of the full Shuttle
NEE distribution to prevent extreme values from compressing the colour range.
Site-years with no NEE data are shown as grey hexbins.

Inset text shows the dataset label, number of sites, and total site-years with
valid NEE data.

Data sources:
- Flux data: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, April 2026 (672 sites downloaded,
  530 with valid NEE_VUT_REF after QC gating on NEE_VUT_REF_QC >= 0.50)
- Climate data: WorldClim v2.1 (Fick, S.E. and R.J. Hijmans, 2017.
  WorldClim 2: new 1-km spatial resolution climate surfaces for global
  land areas. International Journal of Climatology 37(12): 4302-4315)
