Figure 5 — FLUXNET site distribution in Whittaker biome climate space, current network (530 sites with valid NEE). X axis: mean annual temperature (°C, WorldClim v2.1 bio1, 1970-2000 baseline, 2.5 arc-minute resolution). Y axis: mean annual precipitation (mm yr⁻¹, WorldClim v2.1 bio12). Hexagonal bins show median annual NEE (g C m⁻² yr⁻¹). Diverging colour scale centred on zero, clipped to 5th-95th percentile. Horizontal legend bar. Data sources: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7; WorldClim v2.1 (Fick & Hijmans 2017).
