Figure 5 — FLUXNET site distribution in Whittaker biome climate space (current network, all 530 sites with valid NEE). X axis: mean annual temperature (°C, WorldClim v2.1 bio1, 1970-2000 baseline, 2.5 arc-minute resolution). Y axis: mean annual precipitation (mm yr⁻¹, WorldClim v2.1 bio12). Hexagonal bins show median annual NEE (g C m⁻² yr⁻¹) across all site-years within each climate bin. Diverging colour scale centred on zero: blue = carbon sink, red = carbon source. NEE values clipped to 5th-95th percentile range to improve colour contrast. Data sources: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7 processed data; WorldClim v2.1 (Fick & Hijmans 2017, Int J Climatol).
