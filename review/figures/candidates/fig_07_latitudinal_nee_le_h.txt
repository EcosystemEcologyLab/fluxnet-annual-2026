Figure 7 — Latitudinal Distribution of Annual Carbon and Energy Fluxes

This figure shows annual NEE, Latent Heat Flux (LE), and Sensible Heat Flux (H)
binned by 5-degree latitude bands across the FLUXNET network.

Each panel shows the min-max range as a ribbon and site-mean flux as points
per latitude band. Panels from top to bottom: NEE (g C m⁻² yr⁻¹), LE (W m⁻²),
H (W m⁻²). Points are coloured by IGBP class.

Based on 530 sites with valid NEE_VUT_REF after QC (NEE_VUT_REF_QC threshold
= 0.50, gating on primary variable only).

Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, April 2026.
Function: fig_latitudinal_multi() in R/figures/fig_latitudinal.R
