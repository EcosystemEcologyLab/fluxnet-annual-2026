Figure 7 — Latitudinal distribution of annual carbon and energy fluxes across the FLUXNET network. Each panel shows the min-max range as a ribbon and site-mean flux as points by 5° latitude band. Top: Net Ecosystem Exchange (NEE, g C m⁻² yr⁻¹, positive = source). Middle: Latent Heat Flux (LE, W m⁻²). Bottom: Sensible Heat Flux (H, W m⁻²). Points shaped by IGBP class. Based on 530 sites with valid NEE_VUT_REF after QC (NEE_VUT_REF_QC threshold = 0.50). Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7 processed data, April 2026.
