Figure 8 — Annual carbon and energy flux response to ERA5 climate gradients across the FLUXNET network. Each panel shows binned median flux (line) with IQR ribbon (shaded) and individual site-year observations (grey points) across 15 equal-frequency climate bins. Rows: Net Ecosystem Exchange (NEE_VUT_REF, g C m⁻² yr⁻¹), Latent Heat Flux (LE_F_MDS, W m⁻²), Sensible Heat Flux (H_F_MDS, W m⁻²). Columns: Mean Annual Air Temperature (TA_ERA, ERA5, °C), Annual Precipitation (P_ERA, ERA5, mm yr⁻¹), Vapour Pressure Deficit (VPD_ERA, ERA5, kPa), Aridity Index (CGIAR Global Aridity Index v3.1, MAP/PET, Zomer et al. 2022). Coloured lines show IGBP classes with ≥20 site-years. Based on 530 sites with valid NEE after QC. Data sources: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7; ERA5 climate variables extracted per site; CGIAR Global Aridity Index v3.1 (doi:10.6084/m9.figshare.7504448.v5).
