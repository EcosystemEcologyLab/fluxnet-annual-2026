Figure 8 — Annual carbon and energy flux response to ERA5 climate gradients. 3×4 grid: rows = NEE/LE/H, columns = Mean Annual Temperature (°C), Annual Precipitation (mm yr⁻¹), VPD (kPa), Aridity Index. Binned median flux with IQR ribbon per IGBP class (≥20 site-years). Individual site-year observations as grey background points. Axis limits clipped: T > -5°C, P < 4000 mm, VPD < 2 kPa, AI < 2.5. IGBP legend in bottom-right panel. Data sources: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7; ERA5; CGIAR Global Aridity Index v3.1 (Zomer et al. 2022).
