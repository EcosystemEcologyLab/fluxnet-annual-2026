Median annual NEE (NEE_VUT_REF, with NEE_CUT_REF fallback for CUT-only sites) time series with 95% CI ribbon, faceted by IGBP class. Produced by fig_flux_timeseries_by_igbp().

Pipeline: DuckDB canonical (commit 21a2ca3).
