Annual NEE by IGBP class across equal-width time bins

Boxplots of annual NEE by IGBP class, stratified into 10-year time bins to show temporal change. Produced by fig_flux_by_igbp_timeslice().

Annual data: 6,108 FLUXMET site-years after QC.
Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, 759 sites, Jun 1 2026 snapshot (TERN-inclusive).
QC: NEE_VUT_REF_QC >= 0.50 (VUT sites); NEE_CUT_REF_QC >= 0.50 (CUT-only sites, per-site fallback, commit ad7464f).
Pipeline: DuckDB canonical (commit 21a2ca3).
Function: R/figures/fig_igbp.R — fig_flux_by_igbp_timeslice()
