Global map of long-term mean NEE (sites with >= 5 years)

Sites coloured by long-term mean annual NEE (NEE_VUT_REF, with NEE_CUT_REF fallback for CUT-only sites) using a blue-grey-red diverging scale centred on zero. Only sites with >= 5 valid years shown. Produced by fig_map_nee_mean().

Annual data: 6,108 FLUXMET site-years after QC.
Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, 759 sites, Jun 1 2026 snapshot (TERN-inclusive).
QC: NEE_VUT_REF_QC >= 0.50 (VUT sites); NEE_CUT_REF_QC >= 0.50 (CUT-only sites, per-site fallback, commit ad7464f).
Pipeline: DuckDB canonical (commit 21a2ca3).
Function: R/figures/fig_maps.R — fig_map_nee_mean()
