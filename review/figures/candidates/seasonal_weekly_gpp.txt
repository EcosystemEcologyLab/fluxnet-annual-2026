Weekly median seasonal cycle of GPP by IGBP class

ISO-week median GPP_NT_VUT_REF per IGBP class with top/right mirror axes. Only IGBP classes with >= 3 sites included. Produced by fig_seasonal_weekly().

Daily data: ~2M FLUXMET site-days after FLUXMET filter and NEE NA removal.
Data source: FLUXNET Shuttle v0.3.7, 759 sites, Jun 1 2026 snapshot (TERN-inclusive).
QC: NEE_VUT_REF_QC >= 0.50 (VUT sites); NEE_CUT_REF_QC >= 0.50 (CUT-only sites, per-site fallback, commit ad7464f).
Pipeline: DuckDB canonical (commit 21a2ca3).
Function: R/figures/fig_seasonal.R — fig_seasonal_weekly()
