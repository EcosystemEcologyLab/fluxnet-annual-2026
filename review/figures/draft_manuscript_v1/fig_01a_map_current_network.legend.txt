FIGURE LEGEND — fig_03_map_current.png
========================================

TITLE: Global distribution of the current FLUXNET Shuttle network

DESCRIPTION:
Global point map showing the location of every eddy covariance flux tower
site in the current FLUXNET network. Each site is plotted as a single
uniformly-coloured point at its registered latitude/longitude; no
classification, colour-coding, or size-scaling of points is applied — the
figure is a simple presence map. An outline-only basemap (country borders,
light grey land fill) provides geographic context. No plot title or
subtitle is rendered; site count is reported here rather than on the figure
itself.

AXES:
No coordinate axes are drawn (map uses a void theme — no tick marks, tick
labels, or axis titles). The map is unprojected longitude/latitude
(equirectangular), cropped to y ∈ [-56°, 85°] to remove empty ocean south of
the southernmost sites while retaining Australia, New Zealand, southern
Africa, and southern South America. Because the figure canvas is square
(3.5 × 3.5 in) while an unclipped global extent is markedly wider than tall
(~2.55:1), the map itself renders centred with white space above and below
— an accepted tradeoff of the single-column square format, not missing data.

POINT STYLING:
Shape 21 (filled circle with outline), fill colour #0072B2 (Okabe-Ito blue,
colour-blind-safe), black outline, size 2.0 pt, stroke 0.4, alpha 0.9.
Colour is constant — it does not encode IGBP, hub, or any other variable.

NETWORK AND SITE COUNT:
FLUXNET Shuttle network, current development-mode snapshot: n = 767 sites.
This figure shows the Shuttle network only — it does not overlay or compare
against any historical dataset (Marconi, La Thuile, FLUXNET2015). A separate
companion figure (fig_04_map_snapshots.png, same source script) shows those
three historical networks alongside the current Shuttle network as an
explicitly-labelled four-panel comparison; that figure is not part of this
draft-manuscript set.

DATA SOURCE:
Site coordinates (location_lat, location_long) and site_id from the FLUXNET
Shuttle snapshot CSV data/snapshots/fluxnet_shuttle_snapshot_20260624T095651.csv
(767 sites), obtained via flux_listall()/flux_download() per the project's
Shuttle-only primary-data policy (CLAUDE.md §1). No FLUXNET2015, AmeriFlux,
or ICOS-FLUXNET data is used in this figure.

EXCLUSIONS:
Sites with missing or out-of-range coordinates (location_lat outside
[-90, 90] or location_long outside [-180, 180], or either value NA) are
dropped before plotting. Duplicate site_id rows are collapsed to one point
per site. No sites were excluded on these grounds in the current snapshot;
all 767 sites are shown.

BASEMAP:
Country/coastline outlines from rnaturalearth::ne_countries(scale =
"medium", 1:50m resolution), land fill grey97 (near-white), border colour
black, s2 spherical geometry disabled for the crop/render step to avoid
outline rendering artefacts.

REPRODUCIBILITY:
Function: fig_map_point_network() in R/figures/fig_maps.R
Script:   scripts/generate_point_maps.R
Output:   review/figures/maps_point/fig_03_map_current.png, copied to
          review/figures/candidates/fig_03_map_current.png
DIMENSIONS: 3.5 × 3.5 inches, 300 dpi, white background
