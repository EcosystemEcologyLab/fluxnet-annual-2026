FIGURE LEGEND — fig_05_whittaker_current.png
==============================================

TITLE: Whittaker climate-space distribution of the current FLUXNET network,
coloured by median annual net ecosystem exchange

DESCRIPTION:
Hexagonal-binned scatter plot placing every current FLUXNET Shuttle site in
Whittaker climate space (mean annual temperature × mean annual
precipitation). Each hexagonal bin is coloured by the median annual net
ecosystem exchange (NEE) observed across all site-years falling within that
climate-space bin; bins with no observed flux data are left uncoloured
(NA, not drawn). Individual site locations are additionally overplotted as
semi-transparent grey points so that sampling density within each hexagon is
visible alongside the flux colour. No biome-boundary polygons are drawn —
despite the "Whittaker" name (a reference to the classic biome diagram this
plot type is based on), this implementation shows only the hexbin/point
climate distribution, not biome classification outlines. No plot title is
rendered.

AXES:
  X — Mean Annual Temperature (°C), fixed range -15 to 35
  Y — Mean Annual Precipitation (mm yr⁻¹), fixed range 0 to 4000

Four-sided black tick marks (inward) are drawn on all axes via a duplicated
secondary axis with no labels. Panel has a solid black border and no
gridlines.

COLOUR SCALE:
Diverging blue–white–red scale (colorspace "Blue-Red 3"), centred on
NEE = 0 (white), blue = net carbon uptake (negative NEE), red = net carbon
release (positive NEE). Colour limits are computed from the 5th–95th
percentile of the *full*, unfiltered annual Shuttle NEE distribution (not
just the sites/years shown in this panel), so the scale is directly
comparable across the family of Whittaker figures this same script produces
for other network snapshots (Marconi, La Thuile, FLUXNET2015, and Shuttle
sub-snapshots at 2000/2007/2015) — none of which are part of this
draft-manuscript figure set.

BINNING:
Hexagonal binning via ggplot2::stat_summary_hex(), 15 bins along each axis;
summary statistic per bin is the median of per-site median annual NEE values
(median-of-medians, not a flux-weighted or site-year-weighted mean).

NETWORK AND SITE COUNT:
FLUXNET Shuttle network, current development-mode snapshot: N = 767 sites;
inset text on the figure reports N = 767 sites | 4,236 site-years — the
site-year count reflects only site-years with a valid climate match and a
finite annual NEE value (see Exclusions).

DATA SOURCES:
  - Site list: current snapshot
    data/snapshots/fluxnet_shuttle_snapshot_20260624T095651.csv (767 sites)
  - Climate (MAT/MAP): WorldClim v2.1, 2.5 arc-minute BIO1 (annual mean
    temperature) and BIO12 (annual precipitation), pre-extracted per site in
    data/snapshots/site_worldclim.csv, with on-the-fly terra::extract() from
    the WorldClim GeoTIFFs as a fallback for any site not already in that
    table
  - Flux data: annual_converted table in the project's DuckDB database
    (data/duckdb/fluxnet.duckdb), FLUXMET dataset, after QC filtering
    (04_qc.R) and unit conversion (05_units.R); NEE_VUT_REF is used with a
    coalesce fallback to NEE_CUT_REF for the ~36 CUT-only sites that carry no
    VUT-partitioned value, per the project's per-site QC fallback rule
    (CLAUDE.md — QC Flag Reference)

EXCLUSIONS:
Sites lacking a WorldClim climate match, or with no finite annual NEE value
after the VUT/CUT coalesce, are dropped from both the hexbin summary and the
overlaid points (retained in the site-year and colour-scale denominators
only where noted above). No IGBP or other classification filter is applied.

REPRODUCIBILITY:
Function: fig_whittaker_worldclim() in R/figures/fig_climate.R
Script:   scripts/generate_whittaker.R (figure "Whit01" in that script's
          numbered sequence of 9 Whittaker figures)
Output:   review/figures/whittaker/fig_whit01_ShuttleFull.png, copied to
          review/figures/candidates/fig_05_whittaker_current.png
DIMENSIONS: 3.5 × 3.5 inches, 300 dpi, white background
